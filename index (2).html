ASTA STARIA

To compensate for having no magic, since childhood Asta has intensely trained his body to have incredible physical strength. This strength allows him to wield the heavy anti-magic swords, which allow him to cancel out magic.

NOELLE SILVA

Noelle is a water mage. Born with incredible magic potential as a royal, she is initially unable to properly control her magic, with it veering to different directions.

NACHT FAUST

Nacht Faust is a character from the popular Japanese manga and anime series Black Clover. He is the current Vice-Captain of the Black Bull squad, a notorious squad of the Clover Kingdom's Magic Knights. Nacht is known for his enigmatic and mysterious personality, as well as his powerful and versatile magic abilities.

ZORA IDEALE

Zora is shown to be highly disrespectful towards all sorts of people that he stumbles upon including Nobles, the Magic Emperor, and even his allies.

YAMI SUKEHIRO

Dark Magic: Yami uses this rare magic attribute to generate and manipulate the element of darkness. He primarily uses this magic by channeling it into his katana.
